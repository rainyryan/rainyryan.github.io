Supplemental Health Insurance

Life doesn't stop when a medical event happens. Supplemental health insurance provides cash/indemnity benefits paid directly to you to help with deductibles, copays, and everyday out-of-pocket costs.

Whether it's rent, groceries, or travel for care, you can use the money however you need it most.

Why it matters:

  • Benefits paid regardless of other coverage
  • Portable coverage that stays with you
